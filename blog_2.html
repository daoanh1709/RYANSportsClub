Best Mushroom Recipes to Try

December 18, 2020

Mushrooms are a severely underrated vegetable. They’re often hiding in the background, playing the supporting role instead of having their time in the limelight. But mushrooms are a powerhouse, adding serious meatiness and deep, rich flavor everywhere they’re added. You can count on this regardless of variety. Shiitakes, oysters, trumpet, and morel mushrooms always get high marks, but even basic button mushrooms have a lot to offer up.

Chicken thigh mushroom mixed with satay

  1. Chicken thigh mushrooms are washed with salt water and then tear the warp into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Boil the pot of water, let the mushrooms boil for 2-3 minutes, the mushrooms are just cooked, then north down, take out the basket to dry.
  3. Crushed and chopped garlic. Add mushrooms, minced garlic, satay, vinegar, roasted sesame seeds, powdered wings, ground pepper into a large bowl, mix well.
  4. Put soy sauce, sesame oil, celery into the above mixture, mix again, leave for about 5-10 minutes to absorb spices.

Young tofu with shiitake mushroom sauce

  1. Shiitake mushrooms cut the old leg, rinse, soak in dilute salt water for about 15 minutes to clean. Then picked out thin slices to eat. Wash young tofu, slice it to taste, then steam it.
  2. Put the pan on the stove, add 1 teaspoon of sesame oil. When the oil is hot, add the top part of the onion to the top and fry it. Next, add the mushrooms and stir-fry, add a little vegetarian oyster oil, soy sauce to stir thoroughly. Mix 1 teaspoon of flour with a little water, stir in a pan of mushrooms, season to taste.
  3. Finally, add the mushrooms with the sauce above onto the cooked young tofu plate. Use a spoon to evenly pour the sauce over the beans so that the tofu is evenly absorbed. So the food is done and ready to be enjoyed.

Vegetarian mushroom spring rolls

  1. Root cut fungus, wash off. Shredded chicken thigh mushrooms. Minced fresh shiitake mushrooms. The needle mushrooms are poached in boiling water for a little while.
  2. Mix bean sprouts with mushroom mixture, season with 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon of pepper. After that, use rice paper and roll it into bite-sized rolls.
  3. Put 1 pan of oil on the stove, hot oil for each spring roll and fry. After frying, you mix vegetarian fish sauce to eat with spring rolls, you can use vegetarian fish sauce prepared at the market, add garlic and chili as you like.

Crispy fried mushroom

  1. Cut root needle mushroom, separate the fibers (1-2 strands / branch), wash them.
  2. The flour is dissolved with water into a thick paste, then let the dough rest for about 30 minutes.
  3. Break the eggs into the bowl of flour, season with salt, pepper the flour and then beat.
  4. Heat the pan of cooking oil, dip the mushrooms in the dough, then put in the frying pan until golden brown.
