WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN THE LEEP ASIA 2020 EVENT WITH US?

Following the success of LEEP ASIA 2019, LEEP ASIA 2020 introduces many new activities, full of surprises and attractions. Here are a few reasons why we cannot ignore this important event.

LEEP ASIA 2018 is expected to take place in Ho Chi Minh City. Ho Chi Minh City, an exhibition event in the field of fitness, entertainment and aesthetics with the participation of many leading brands in the fields of health, sports and fitness, is expected to attract thousands of participants. attend. The event also brings together trends, technologies and innovations related to industries that enhance the quality of life, including media, sports and entertainment, equipment and products. nutrition, medical services provided for longevity and beauty.

1. Many interesting and interesting activities going on in 04 consecutive days

LEEP ASIA 2020 is expected to be held in August 2020 at Nguyen Du Stadium for 04 consecutive days with diverse and attractive activities indoors and outdoors. Not only exhibiting the largest scale of fitness, entertainment and aesthetics in Vietnam, LEEP ASIA 2020 also brings many other rich activities that are expected to attract the attention of many different subjects. such as fashion show, CMG.ASIA Fitness & Entertainment Award ceremony, Group X sports practice, Muay Thai competition, PT-X Summer Fitness Challenge 2020 final, Food Contest, outdoor volleyball competition,...

LEEP ASIA 2020 is expected to be held in August 2020 at Nguyen Du Stadium for 04 consecutive days with diverse and attractive activities indoors and outdoors. (Photo: CFYC)

2. Renowned foreign experts attend LEEP ASIA 2020:

Also in this LEEP ASIA, fans who love fitness in Vietnam will have the opportunity to buy tickets to attend the Musclecontest Vietnam 2020 final, under the scoring of prestigious and famous International Class referee. : Mr. Tamer El-Guindy. The President of the International Fitness Competition (Musclecontest International) - Tamer El-Guindy will come to Vietnam to meet and interact with fans. Besides, he will also be one of the judges of the Vietnamese version of Musclecontest.

Up to now, Tamer has been in the bodybuilding industry for 20 years. He went from the smallest positions such as Personal Coach Manager, Assistant, International Business Consulting, etc. to the leadership positions such as President of Musclecontest, Director of franchise at Gold's Gym or vice president of franchising worldwide at UFC Gym. The season of experience, not afraid of work and hardships has made Tamer a successful leader in his favorite field.

The President of the International Bodybuilding Competition (Musclecontest) - Tamer El-Guindy will come to Vietnam to meet and interact with fans. (Photo: Per Bernal)

3. The participation of Hochiminh City Wings and Saigon Heat

The good news for LEEP ASIA 2020: The fans not only have the opportunity to watch the handsome guys of the two clubs on the competition, but also have the opportunity to interact with their favorite athletes. Hochiminh City Wings and Saigon Heat are two basketball clubs that are admired by many young people today.

Saigon Heat vs Hochiminh City Wings will attend LEEP ASIA 2020. (Image: VBA)

Hochiminh City Wings is a professional basketball team based in Ho Chi Minh City. The team attended the first professional basketball tournament in Vietnam VBA 2016.

Saigon Heat, with its experience in building a professional sports model and constantly improving, played a leading role in the establishment of the first professional basketball tournament of Vietnam in 2016. VBA quickly turned into a whirlwind attracting basketball fans nationwide, becoming a nursery for young talents, as well as a springboard to raise the level of Vietnamese basketball on the world arena.

4. The special guests

One of the factors that make this event attractive is the participation of artists and guests who have influence on the fitness and entertainment field in Vietnam. In particular, there will be many famous faces of the entertainment industry during the past year who have been engaged in exercise and beauty at the CMG Group's Clubs and Beauty Institutes. ASIA will appear at the CMG Fitness awards night and Entertainment Awards on August 17, 2020, such as: Ho Ngoc Ha, Thu Minh, Dam Vinh Hung,...

Singer Ho Ngoc Ha will attend this year's awards night

In particular, at Yoga For Life night, in addition to the presence of famous masters from the cradle of Yoga, India, the program also had a special appearance of two Vietnamese Yoga followers: singer Ho Ngoc Ha and singer Jolie Phuong Trinh.

LEEP ASIA 2018 will have the appearance of many famous artists. (Photo: BTC)

LEEP ASIA 2020 is not only an opportunity for CMG.ASIA to look back on the past, thank the members who have been closely with the group for a long time, but also a step to continue to grow and increase growth in the future.
