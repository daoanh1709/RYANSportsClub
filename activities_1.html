“MY CLUB - MY ADDICTION” PACKAGES CONFIGURATION OF ADDICTION

Many Vietnamese people are choosing to "add" to things that harm their health and cause cancer such as alcohol intolerance, stimulants... "My Club - My Addiction" contributes to reshape behavior "addiction". You are still completely "addicted" to things that bring health benefits.

There are three common addictions that cause the top three cancers today are smoking addiction that causes lung cancer, alcoholism leads to liver cancer and eating bad products that negatively affect the stomach. According to a recent cancer prevention study, an effective cancer prevention triangle can reduce the risk of the disease by up to 30% if done regularly. The first is regular exercise, the second is the scientific diet, the third is weight control.

With the above researches, CMG.ASIA group constantly focuses on activities to create awareness for the Vietnamese people in enhancing the body's resistance by physical and mental activities. The practical deities at group clubs are as follows:

1. Establishing CMG Family Cancer Foundation - Fund to support cancer patients, especially children and their relatives. All revenue earned from CMG.ASIA Group's operations will be partly deducted as operating expenses for this fund.

2. The LEEP ASIA Life Quality Enhancement Exhibition, which took place in mid-August, is also part of the desire to increase awareness of the Vietnamese people about cancer. "LEEP ASIA is expected by me as a media push affecting the psychology of all Vietnamese people that sports addiction will help us live healthier lives" said Randy Dobson - President of CMG.ASIA.

3. In September 2018, CMG.ASIA Group introduced the program BTC21 - Body Transformation Challenge in order to motivate members and customers to change their face positively through improving the Bodybuilding stats: muscle gain, fat loss. Controlling weight and having a healthy body contributes to good and sustainable health and prevention of diseases, including dangerous cancers.

4. Also in September 2018, CMG.ASIA group introduced Move 21 campaign; Challenge yourself and your friends for 21 consecutive days. 21 days form a routine, thereby helping the body maintain exercise and active lifestyle - a factor that contributes to the prevention and reduction of the risk of cancer. After 21 days, the group will help each member donate 210,000 VND to the CMG Family Cancer Foundation.

5. On Christmas 2018 this year, CMG.ASIA will also organize the Xmas Giving Tree activity. This is an annual activity aimed at connecting members and members of CMG.ASIA to join hands to help children. This year, the program will target children who are being treated for cancer.
